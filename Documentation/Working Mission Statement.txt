Working Methodocracy Mission Statement:

To develop and use the best method for solving problems. To improve upon the logical thought process of the scientific method. To ensure that everyone can participate in the debate, and the method's development.

To increase the methods' robustness, efficiency, effectiveness, and benefit by subjecting the designs to it's own method.

To bring these methods to the efficient platform of the internet. To keep the process open to everyone and only judge based on the merit of an argument. To improve these methods by subjecting their design to themselves, and to increase the systems' robustness, efficiency, and effectiveness.
