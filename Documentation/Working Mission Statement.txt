To continually improve the best methods for problem-solving, using the scientific method as a basis, starting by digitizing it into a knowledge base accessed and developed by users through the internet. To apply this process to areas in which it is not being applied. To ensure that everyone can participate in the method, a sort of debate, access the version history of the knowledge base, improve the method's design using the method itself, and contribute to the open-sourced development of the code.
